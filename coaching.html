Performance and Mindset Coaching

I work with people
who want to
achieve their dreams.

Performance coaching for senior leaders and athletes who have reached the level where skill is no longer the bottleneck. What holds you back now lives inside you, not on your CV.

Who this is for

You have done the hard work
to get here. Now what?

🏢
Senior Leaders
You are technically excellent and you have proven it. But somewhere between the promotion and today, the game changed. The problems are less clear. The stakes are higher. The people around you look to you for answers you are not always sure you have.
You second-guess decisions you would have made confidently two years ago
You know what you should do but struggle to hold the line under pressure
You lead others well but find it harder to lead yourself

Athletes
You train the body. You work the tactics. But the difference between good and great at the level you are playing at is almost entirely what happens in your mind before, during, and after the game.
You perform well in training but something shifts when it counts
One bad decision can derail your whole game
You carry mistakes longer than you should
You want a mental edge that compounds over time, not just a pep talk

What makes this different

This is not advice.
It is a process.

Most people who call themselves coaches give you their answer. They tell you what worked for them, or what they read in a book, and they package it as wisdom.

I do not give you answers. I help you find your own. Because the answer you discover yourself is the one you will actually use. The one someone else gives you is the one you nod at and forget.

I learned this on the pitch. You cannot referee for someone else. You cannot make the decision for the player under pressure. You can only build the conditions inside them so that when the moment arrives, they are ready for it.

That is what I bring to every coaching relationship. Not a framework. Not a methodology with a name. A genuine belief that you already have more than you think you have. My job is to help you access it.

15 years of leadership under pressure
Not theory. I have built teams from scratch, co-founded a startup, coached at national finals, and navigated the moments where things go wrong. I bring all of that into every session.
Sport and business in the same room
The mental skills that make a great match official are the same ones that make a great leader. I move fluently between both worlds because I live in both of them.
Honest, not comfortable
I will ask you the question you are hoping I do not ask. That is not cruelty. That is care. The uncomfortable question is usually the one that moves things forward.
You set the agenda
We work on what is actually in your way, not a pre-packaged curriculum. Every session starts with where you are right now.

"Getting people to find their answer empowers them into feeling stronger and more confident. Then they can actually practice their new mindset. That is the difference between a session that lasts a week and one that changes how you think."

How it works

Simple. Personal. Honest.

01
A conversation first
We start with a no-pressure conversation. You tell me where you are and what you are trying to work on. I tell you honestly whether I think I can help. No sales pitch. No package presented before we have even spoken.
02
We find what is actually in the way
The presenting problem is rarely the real problem. Before we try to fix anything, we spend time understanding what is actually going on. That takes honesty from you and curiosity from me. Both are non-negotiable.
03
We build from the inside out
Sustainable performance comes from self-awareness, not technique. We work on the internal conditions first. Then we look at behaviour, decisions, and habits. In that order, not the reverse.

Where this comes from

I have been doing this
for a long time.
Just not always with that name.

For almost a decade I have been coaching match officials for Football Australia, including the 2024 Australia Cup Final and the 2025 Australian Championship Grand Final. The work I do pitchside is performance and mindset coaching. I just never called it that.

I also co-founded RefCoach, a global referee coaching community that reached 5,000 members and delivered mindset performance workshops to over 10,000 referees worldwide in partnership with the Referees' Association of England.

In the tech world, I have led and rebuilt teams across startups and enterprise, always applying the same coaching philosophy. The Deakin University team I inherited had two people returning from stress leave and twelve months of leadership instability. Within six months they outperformed the entire university on engagement by 10%. That is not a process result. That is a people result.

I am currently working toward formal ICF accreditation through the Diploma of Life Coaching at The Life Coaching College, Melbourne. This is the direction I am heading. If you want to work with someone at the start of that journey rather than at the polished, packaged end of it, I am genuinely your person.
